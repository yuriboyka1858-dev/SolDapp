Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Fear & Greed Swap. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains our data collection practices.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

Fear & Greed Swap is a decentralized, non-custodial interface. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect, store, or share any personal identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, IP address, or physical location.

3. Blockchain Data

When you use our interface to connect your Solana wallet and execute swaps, your transactions are broadcasted to the public Solana blockchain. This data is publicly visible and permanent. We do not control this public data and cannot delete it.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics that track your behavior across other websites.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
